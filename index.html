Mehndi 1pm-3pm

Mehndi, also known as henna, is a traditional Indian wedding ceremony that involves applying a red-orange paste made from henna leaves to the bride's hands and feet. The ceremony is usually held the night before the wedding and is organized by the bride's family and friends. The paste is believed to symbolize good luck, wealth, health, and positive spirits, and is said to cool the bride's nerves and keep her calm.

Cocktail Night 8pm-10pm

Cocktail parties usually happen a day or two before the wedding and are a great way for the groom and bride's family to mingle, get to know each other and build new relationships. Said parties usually include a grand display of cocktails and mocktails, mouth-watering hors d'oeuvres and delicious finger foods, all served in an opulent setting. The décor can range from elegant to funky, with a particular emphasis on lighting and music to create a lively and upbeat atmosphere.

Haldi 12pm-5pm

Like the sangeet and mehndi, the haldi is a prewedding ritual common at traditional Indian weddings. "The haldi ceremony is a significant prewedding event and ritual in almost every Indian regional wedding," explains Sen. "It is celebrated on the wedding day or one day prior to the wedding day and is usually a close-knitted event. The closest family, relatives and friends come together on this auspicious occasion to apply turmeric paste (haldi) on the bride and groom's face and body.

Wedding 8pm-10pm

An Indian wedding is a vibrant celebration filled with rituals, colors, and traditions. It typically spans several days, beginning with pre-wedding ceremonies like the Mehendi (henna) and Sangeet (music and dance). The wedding day features the Baarat, where the groom arrives on a decorated horse or vehicle, followed by the exchange of garlands and the sacred rituals around the holy fire, known as the Saat Phere, symbolizing the couple’s vows. The event is marked by elaborate attire, sumptuous feasts, and joyous music, uniting not just the couple but also their families in a festive atmosphere.
